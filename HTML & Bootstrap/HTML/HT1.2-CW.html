Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish made with marinated & grilled chicken (Tandoori chicken), simmered in a creamy tomato gravy/curry.

Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish popular in South Asia, made with rice, meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef), prawn or fish and spices.

  1. Basmati Rice
  2. Chicken or Lamb (or vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for flavor and color

Dosa

A dosa, dosey, dosai, dosha is a thin, savoury crepe in Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice.
