Overview

Virat Kohli is a former international cricketer and the current captain of the India national cricket team in men's cricket and the ICC Men's T20 World Cup team.

Kohli is a right-handed batsman who plays for Delhi in domestic cricket, and for the India national team in international cricket. He is the highest run scorer in ODI and Test cricket for India, having the most runs in both formats combined.
